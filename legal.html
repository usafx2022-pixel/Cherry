This Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how it is stored and protected, and what rights you have over it. We handle pharmacy and patient data with the care that sensitive health information demands.

1. Who This Policy Covers

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • Clients — pharmacy businesses that subscribe to the Platform;
  • Users — staff members who access the Platform under a Client's account;
  • Patients — individuals whose data is entered into the Platform by pharmacy staff during the course of dispensing.

The Provider acts as a data processor on behalf of Clients. Clients are the data controllers responsible for ensuring they have lawful grounds to process patient data and that they comply with applicable data protection legislation in their jurisdiction (including but not limited to GDPR, HIPAA, POPIA, or local health data regulations).

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account and Profile Data

  • Name, email address, and role of each User;
  • Organisation name, branch details, and subscription information.

2.2 Operational Data

  • Inventory records (drug names, quantities, expiry dates, costs);
  • Sales transactions and receipts;
  • Purchase orders and supplier information;
  • Staff shift records;
  • Return and refund records.

2.3 Patient Data (entered by pharmacy staff)

  • Customer names and loyalty records (where used);
  • Prescription references and dispensing records;
  • Insurance claim information (where applicable).

We do not directly collect patient data — it is entered by pharmacy staff using the Platform. Clients are responsible for obtaining appropriate consent from patients and for the accuracy of data entered.

2.4 Technical Data

  • Authentication tokens and session data;
  • Browser type and device information (collected by Supabase Auth and Cloudflare);
  • Audit log entries (user actions, timestamps).

3. How We Use Data

We use collected data solely to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Platform;
  • Authenticate users and maintain session security;
  • Generate reports and analytics visible only to the relevant Client;
  • Communicate with Clients about their subscription and platform updates;
  • Investigate and resolve support issues.

We do not sell, rent, or share Client Data or patient data with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

4. Data Storage and Infrastructure

The Platform is built on Supabase (database and authentication) hosted on AWS infrastructure, and Netlify (frontend hosting). Data is stored in encrypted databases. Data at rest and in transit is encrypted using industry-standard protocols (TLS/SSL, AES-256).

Clients should be aware that data may be stored on servers located outside their home country. Where this is a concern under local regulations, Clients should consult their legal counsel before using the Platform.

5. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: data is retained for as long as the subscription is active;
  • After subscription cancellation: data is retained for 30 days to allow export, then permanently deleted;
  • Audit logs: retained for 12 months for security and compliance purposes.

6. Security Measures

We implement the following security controls:

  • Row-Level Security (RLS) — each Client's data is isolated at the database level and is inaccessible to other Clients;
  • Role-based access control — staff see only the data their role permits;
  • Optional Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) for all user accounts;
  • Automatic session timeout after 10 minutes of inactivity;
  • Cloudflare Turnstile bot protection on the login screen;
  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS) for all data in transit.

No system is completely immune to security threats. Clients are encouraged to enable 2FA, use strong passwords, and train staff on safe credential handling.
